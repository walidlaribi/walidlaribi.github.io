Gmail Shelfie, revealed!

Today, is April 1st and usual Google has announced many April fools.

I personally liked the Gmail Shelfie, it’s just cool. Each time you open your email you could see some selfies (sometimes pretty ones :p). It seemed weird to me, why should Google add such feature to Gmail just for April 1st ??

So I fired up my console I clicked the famous “Inspect Element” on the images to see how it is working, I figured out that they’re just using static images and there is no such a service behind. So let’s discover all the 27 beautiful high resolution slefies that Google made to let us seem stupid by believing it was a new addition to Gmail!
